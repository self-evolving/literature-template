## Summary

The paper introduces the Transformer, a sequence transduction architecture based entirely on attention mechanisms rather than recurrence or convolution.

## Key ideas

- Self-attention lets each token attend directly to other tokens in the sequence.
- Multi-head attention gives the model multiple learned relation spaces.
- Positional encodings provide order information without recurrent state.
