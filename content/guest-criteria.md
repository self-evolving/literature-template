### Hands-on technical experience

Guests should be actively engaged in technical work, such as research, engineering, infrastructure, or product development. We value first-hand experience over purely managerial or commentary roles.

### Human-centered focus

We prioritize work that centers on human needs, values, and collaboration. This can take many forms, including research papers, impactful open-source projects, data infrastructure, or real-world products. We actively welcome diversity across backgrounds, disciplines, and domains.

### Flexible episode format

Episodes can be either *guest-driven* or *topic-driven* tailored to our guest.

- **For guest-driven episodes**, the guest's perspective, experience, and story shape the conversation.
- **For topic-driven episodes**, the primary goal is to explore a specific topic in depth. In this case, finding the *right person* who can talk about the topic matters more than name recognition.
